# Privacy Policy

**Page to Markdown** does not collect, transmit, or store any user data. Ever.

- All processing happens locally in your browser
- No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking
- No network requests are made by this extension
- Page content is never sent to any server
- No personal information is collected or stored

This extension operates entirely offline. The only data processed is the web page you choose to extract, and it never leaves your device.
